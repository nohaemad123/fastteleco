Most Questions


What do I need to start?

FASTteleco cloud invoice system is presented as a service and no installation is required, to start all you need is an internet connection and a browser. Once your account is created, you can begin to create and send your first invoice.

How to create an account?

Registration requires an email address and password. you can sign up from here. You will receive a welcome email inviting you to start your account. you will get 15 days as trial.

And the free version?

The trial account 15 days is not limited and allows new users to start working and to explore the system for free. We invite you to pay the monthly fees if you want use the software for 30 days .

How do I subscribe to it?

The subscription to the service is done within the payment page. then you need to confirm your payment and then our team will activate your account shortly.

How do I pay my subscription?

We accept Credit & Debit card payments online via PayPal. click here to check all the payment methods. If you want to pay with another method please contact us by mail.

And my data?

You can export all your data and make a backup manually too. They also remain accessible at all time even after the end of your subscription.
